Seat Selection Task

Imagine you have just booked the flight shown below for a private trip. You have reached the seat-selection step, where a voice assistant will help you choose your seat.

Please assume that the flight will be fully booked: every seat of that airplane will be occupied by another passenger.

{{ if condition == 'scripted' }}

The assistant will show you {{ C.NUM_ROUNDS }} pairs of seats. For each pair, respond by saying exactly the following sentence:
"Please book Option [A/B] for me."

{{ else }}

The assistant will show you {{ C.NUM_ROUNDS }} pairs of seats. For each pair, say out loud which of the two seats you would like the assistant to book for you. For instance: "I'll take Seat A" or "Please book Option B for me."

{{ endif }}

When you are ready, click the microphone button on the next page and say "Hello" to start.
