Seat Selection Task

Imagine you have just booked the flight shown below for a private trip. You have reached the seat-selection step, where a voice assistant will help you choose your seat.

Please assume that the flight will be fully booked: every seat not selected by you will be occupied by another passenger.

The assistant will show you six pairs of seats. For each pair, simply say out loud which of the two options you prefer. For instance, by saying statements such as "I'd go with Option A" or "The window seat looks better to me" — or anything in between.

When you are ready, click the microphone button on the next page and say "Hello" to start. From the second pair onwards, the assistant will ask you which seat you prefer before showing the map — just answer once the map appears.
